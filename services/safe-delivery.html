Delivery Options

Southeast Safes delivery planning is based on the safe size, property access, pathway condition, floor level, stair conditions, and installation risk.

Store Pickup

Customers may pick up their safe at the dealership at no cost. Store pickup is at the customer’s own risk and does not include loading the product into the vehicle.

Call for details: (321) 777-5625

Ground Floor Delivery

Ground floor delivery is limited to typical first-floor access with 0 to 2 outside stair steps and requires a flat, level concrete or paved pathway.

The safe is removed from the pallet and packaging is removed.

Second Floor Delivery

Second floor delivery may be possible only when the staircase is suitable and manager-approved. It generally applies to 4 to 15 additional interior stair steps.

Stair carry is not available on safes over 36 inches and/or heavier than 600 lbs.

Difficult Delivery Conditions

Difficult terrain, stairways, marble, tile, wood floors, restricted access, multiple floors, or Certificate of Insurance requirements may involve additional charges.

Important Delivery Requirements

Customer Responsibility

The customer is responsible for ensuring the pathway, deck, steps, staircase, and foundation can support the weight of the safe, transporting equipment, and installation crew.

Access Limitations

Some 25 and 35 size safes may not be deliverable due to landing space, staircase structure, or spacing required for stair-climbing equipment.

Risk Review

Southeast Safes reserves the right to decline deliveries or installations involving risk of personal injury or property damage.

Delivery Pricing

Delivery pricing depends on the safe, distance, access, stairs, property conditions, and installation complexity.
